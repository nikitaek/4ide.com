Chat Rules

Welcome to the Chat! This space is designed to provide you with guidance, motivation, and support on your fitness journey. Our team of coaches and consultants (“Coaches”), are here to assist and encourage you in achieving your fitness and nutrition goals, all while enjoying the pleasures of a balanced lifestyle. Before diving into the conversation with your coach, please take a moment to familiarize yourself with the following Chat Rules:

  1. Provide Context: When engaging with our Coaches in the chat, begin by giving a brief but comprehensive overview of your fitness / sleep / well-being journey. Share relevant details about your current health level, fitness level, eating habits, any specific challenges you're facing, and your ultimate goals. The more context our Coaches have, the better they can tailor their advice to your individual circumstances.

  2. Your Confidentiality. Please refrain from disclosing sensitive personal data to our Coaches during your chat sessions. Examples of such data include specific medical conditions or medications, credit card information or bank account numbers, identification numbers, and intimate details. Respect the privacy of others by avoiding the disclosure of sensitive information about third parties, including family members, friends, or colleagues. Your privacy is of utmost importance, and we're here to support you while respecting your confidentiality.

  3. Politeness Matters: Maintain a respectful and polite tone when communicating with the Coaches. Avoid using offensive, indecent, or inappropriate language during your interactions.

  4. Content Etiquette: Do not share explicit, offensive, or inappropriate content in the chat. Avoid spamming the chat with repeated messages or promoting external products or services.

  5. Respectful Conduct: Treat the Coaches with respect. Refrain from engaging in any form of insults, harassment, humiliation, or threats. While our Coaches are here to provide guidance, please refrain from making excessive demands or expecting immediate responses. A positive and supportive environment benefits everyone.

  6. Professional Trust: Recognize the expertise of our Coaches. Avoid questioning or criticizing their professional qualifications. Our Coaches have been carefully selected based on their knowledge and advising skills. By embracing the wealth of experience our Coaches bring to the table, you enhance the effectiveness of the support and advice they provide.

Warning System:

  • Each violation of these rules may result in a warning from us.
  • After the second warning, continued violations may lead to the termination of your access to the chat, with appropriate notification.

Termination Without Warning:

  • We reserve the right to terminate your access to the chat without warnings if you violate our Terms of Service.

Appeals and Questions:

  • If you wish to challenge the termination of your access to the chat, please contact us at 4idecom@gmail.com
